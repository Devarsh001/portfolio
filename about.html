About

I'm a Web Designer and Frontend Developer focused on designing and building modern websites with strong structure, clear content hierarchy, and responsive layout systems.

I work closely with developers to translate design concepts into production-ready interfaces, ensuring that layouts remain consistent across breakpoints and maintain strong usability across devices.

Background

My background combines both design and frontend development. I started working with website builders and WordPress projects, which later led me toward designing structured user interfaces and translating them into responsive layouts.

Today my focus is on improving how websites communicate information through better structure, spacing systems, and clear visual hierarchy.

Design Approach

My design process focuses on building structured interfaces rather than isolated visual elements. Instead of designing pages individually, I focus on establishing repeatable layout patterns, consistent spacing systems, and scalable component structures that can grow as the product evolves.

This approach helps ensure long-term consistency across websites and allows teams to build new pages efficiently while maintaining a cohesive visual system.
