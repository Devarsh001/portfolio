Case Study

Ryan Hlubb Personal Branding Website

Design and development of a professional personal branding website for a commercial real estate valuation expert to establish credibility and communicate his expertise clearly.

Role

Website Designer & Developer

Platform

Custom HTML & CSS

Scope

Personal Branding Website

Project Overview

Ryan Hlubb is a commercial real estate valuation expert who needed a professional online presence to reflect his experience and attract potential clients and collaborators. The goal was to design and build a clean, structured personal website that communicated his background, services, and credibility without unnecessary visual complexity. The final website presents his expertise clearly while maintaining a professional tone consistent with the real estate industry.

The Problem

  • • No existing professional web presence to represent his expertise
  • • No structured way to communicate services and background
  • • Needed a credible, industry-appropriate visual identity
  • • Required a contact pathway for potential clients to reach him

My Role

I was responsible for the full design and development of the website, from initial layout planning in Figma through to the final production build. This included defining the content structure, designing all page layouts, establishing the visual language, and building the responsive frontend from scratch.

Design System Thinking

Even for a single-person personal website, consistent layout patterns and spacing systems were prioritized to ensure the site felt polished and professional throughout every section. Reusable layout blocks were designed so the site could be easily updated or extended in the future without breaking visual consistency.

Layout Structure

Consistent section layouts were used across all pages to maintain a predictable reading flow and professional feel.

Spacing System

A uniform spacing scale was applied throughout to create visual balance and ensure readability across breakpoints.

Reusable Components

Repeatable content blocks including bio sections, service highlights, and contact areas were designed for easy reuse.

Design Approach

The design focused on projecting professionalism and trust — qualities essential for someone operating in commercial real estate. A clean typographic hierarchy, generous white space, and a restrained color palette were used to keep the focus on Ryan's credentials and experience rather than decorative elements. The layout was built with responsiveness as a core requirement, ensuring the site read equally well on desktop and mobile devices.
