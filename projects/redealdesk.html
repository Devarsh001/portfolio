Case Study

ReDealDesk Educational Platform

End-to-end design and development of a structured educational platform presenting commercial real estate decision frameworks in a clear, navigable format.

Role

End-to-End Website Designer

Platform

Custom HTML & CSS

Scope

Educational Platform

Project Overview

ReDealDesk is an educational platform focused on commercial real estate decision-making. The platform needed to present structured frameworks and learning content in a way that was easy to navigate and digest. The challenge was to design a platform that could present dense, information-heavy content clearly without overwhelming the user, while maintaining a consistent visual structure across all sections. The end result is a well-organised educational interface that guides users through real estate concepts in a logical, structured sequence.

The Problem

  • • Complex educational content without a clear presentation structure
  • • No consistent layout system to organise frameworks and modules
  • • Difficulty navigating between related content sections
  • • Content hierarchy needed to guide users through structured learning paths

My Role

I was responsible for the complete design and development of the platform, from content structuring and layout planning through to the final production build. This included defining how complex real estate frameworks would be presented visually, designing the full page system in Figma, and building the responsive frontend to match the approved designs.

Design System Thinking

Educational platforms carry a high volume of content, making a consistent design system critical to usability. Repeatable content modules, consistent section spacing, and a clear typographic hierarchy were established early in the process to ensure the platform remained organised and scannable as content scaled.

Layout Structure

Content modules were built on a consistent grid structure to create a predictable, easy-to-follow reading experience across all framework sections.

Spacing System

A standardised spacing scale was applied throughout to maintain visual breathing room between dense content blocks.

Reusable Components

Framework cards, section headers, and highlight blocks were designed as reusable components to support efficient content scaling.

Design Approach

The design prioritised clarity and structure above aesthetics. Educational content benefits most from clean layouts, strong typographic hierarchy, and clear visual separation between sections. Each framework module was presented using a consistent content pattern, allowing users to build familiarity with the layout and focus their attention on the information rather than navigating the interface. Responsive behaviour was a core consideration, ensuring the platform remained fully usable on both desktop and mobile screen sizes.
